Hi and welcome! :)

If you work as a doctor in the UK's NHS, this website is a free and simple file conversion service that helps you quickly turn your rota, whatever format you receive it in, into a spreadsheet that's ready to import into Google Calendar, Apple/iCloud Calendar, or any other commonly used digital calendar service. This allows you to use a digital calendar for your work rota without having to manually enter every day separately into an app or website.

This website relies on doctors/rota coordinators kindly giving me access to anonymous template rotas for their jobs, using which I can then create specific conversion functions. They are grouped by hospital and specialty/department. If your hospital or department rota is not available, you can request for me to create a converter for your rota.
